# Hassaan Site Vision

## Scope

`hassaan-site` is the canonical public website for Muhammad Hassaan Javed.

It exists to collect and present:

- identity and positioning
- capabilities across disciplines
- selected work and writing
- ecosystem routes into portfolio, tools, products, and shared framework surfaces

It is not the portfolio and it is not the tools hub.

## Contract

- This repo is the public trust anchor for the broader ecosystem.
- The site should stay static-first unless a clear content or product need justifies something heavier.
- Crusher UI Kit provides the design system and shared UI primitives, but the site is free to use whatever stack is best for its own needs.
- The site should prefer structured content and editorial clarity over one giant landing page of unrelated material.
- Portfolio depth belongs in `portfolio.muhammadhassaanjaved.com`.
- Tools and products discovery belongs in `tools.muhammadhassaanjaved.com`.

## Do Not Do

- Do not turn this repo into a general tools directory.
- Do not duplicate the full portfolio here.
- Do not let AI write directly into the public site without a review layer and a structured content model.
- Do not optimize for old implementation convenience over long-term clarity.
